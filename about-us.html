About Us

Welcome to the official site of Hilux Painting and Decorating - a company specialized in all kinds of industrial and residential painting services. Our company consists of highly-experienced house and commercial painters from Sydney who also carry out the jobs in the Sydney suburbs.

We are a team of highly enthusiastic and professional painters and decorators from Sydney. We definitely have an edge over other painting and decorating companies that are working in Sydney because of our vast experience and expertise.

To assure clients’ satisfaction, we always choose the finest materials and tools. This is perhaps the reason that the job we do is going to remain as it is even after a couple of years.

The foremost priority of Hilux Painting and Decorating is to assure the highest quality and competent services to all our clients. Guarantee

Hilux Painting & Decorating have built the reputation on high quality workmanship. We use only the highest quality level of products on today’s markets, we have all the experience in the painting trade giving us the knowledge of what paint is best suited for what surface, and it is because of this we offer you a 10-year workmanship guarantee document prior to any job being started, and will be signed and certified at the end of the job.


Products Used

Hilux Painting & Decorating pride themselves in using only Dulux and Taubmans Premium Paints; all exterior paint is UV resistant, mould proof and is made for Australian conditions. Our internal paints are all mould proof, low VOC, low odour and most importantly, it’s washable.

We have been servicing Sydney for the past 16 years and have built our reputation in the painting industry as quality painters and therefore hold the accreditation as Dulux and Taubmans accredited painters.


Colour Consulting

If your painting job exceeds the value of $5000.00 and you have Hilux Painting & Decorating Ltd the go ahead on the job and a start date has been booked, we will send you out a fully qualified Colour Consultant FREE of charge.

The Colour Consultant will spend an hour with you at you home to ensure you have made the right decision of colour choices for your home. Picking the right colour/ colours can be tricky, stressful and frustrating at times and this is why we offer this service.

Safety

Hilux Painting & Decorating are dedicated to providing its clients with the highest quality surface preparation and coatings application services while providing a safe working environment on all of our projects.

Maintaining a safe and healthy working environment is not only an important human resource issue, it’s the law. Our employees understand health and safety regulations and the steps they need to take to minimize risks.

We adhere to Workplace Safety by implementing the following:

  • Conduct in-house safety programs.
  • All employees to be wearing safety gear such as steel capped boots, hard hats.
  • Gloves & Safety Glasses where required.
  • We clean up daily and remove dangerous tools from premises.
  • Respect of your privacy and only access authorized areas.
  • Crews trained to use products and equipment safely.
  • We carry comprehensive worker's compensation and public liability insurance.
