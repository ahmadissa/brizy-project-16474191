Residential

At Hilux Painting and Decoration, we offer an extensive range of residential painting services in Sydney. First of all, we don’t hire any contractors or middlemen to carry out the task on our behalf. We have our own team of highly skilled, efficient and courteous painters in Sydney, who are proficient in handling any painting project with great precision. Quality work at an affordable price is our main motive, so there is nothing to worry. When you call us, you can expect our painters to come with the latest equipment because we don’t want to waste time using outdated equipment, which are slow and don’t give that desired finish.

How our professionals work

We understand how important it is to get your home painted with proper colors and designs. That’s why our expert painters treat every project individually. We listen to your ideas and blend it with our optical painting techniques to bring out the best results. Hiring our residential painters and decorators will give you a peace of mind.
