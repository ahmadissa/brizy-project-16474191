Rope Access

Hilux Painting and Decoration offers a highly-efficient abseiling service at an extremely affordable price. All Abseil technicians are professionally trained, fully insured and vastly experienced to carry out the rope access job with great precision. We offer a competitive and cost-effective solution to all the residential or commercial buildings that require abseiling service. We are fully aware of the fact that, abseiling service is potentially hazardous, but our experienced and trained individuals are committed to performing such jobs with perfection. When we mentioned ‘trained’, then we mean that they are trained according to IRATA standards (Industrial Rope Access Trade Association). We follow strict health and safety guidelines because we don’t want to risk the lives of our precious employees.

Services provided by us

  1. Repair work.
  2. Painting.
  3. Installation and removal of signs.
  4. Rust removal and treatment.
  5. Location and repair of water leaks.
  6. External window cleaning.
  7. Reglazing needs.

We have a team of approved installers and testers of wire safety devices and eye bolts. Despite the fact that, the abseiling service is full of risks, we are not charging high for it. Our price remains highly affordable, so that people from all backgrounds can hire our services. Rope access service is a highly challenging job, but our experienced employees enjoy it. The foolproof safety that has been provided to them is one of the reasons that they feel so confident while reaching to such extreme heights for carrying out the job. To hire our abseilers, all you need is to call on our phone number. We will be waiting for your call!
